About The Nasty

Origin: Middletown, OH

Styles: Technical, Rythmic, Current

Genres: DNB, Trance, Dubstep, PsyTrance, House


Initially started FL Studio as the Artist Name of 'Slaves To Cornchips' as an EDM Undgerground band-in-garage fitting in the early 2000's. Claiming rise to the local scene during 2012-2013, Nash D took on the alias of DubVinci playing at Dayton, OH locations including 'The Masque' and 'Club Vex' introducing the Dubstep genre to energetic crowds and without apology. In 2013, DJ Nash D created an online Twitch streaming presence with live streams every Friday, 9PM EST


"I started at [the age of] 14 tearing apart technology. Technology is my expertise. I feel that I am becoming more and more skilled as time goes on."
